Letter of Introduction: About Me

I've linked my CV as a Google doc

In fall 2019, I began as an Instructor in the Writing & Rhetoric Department at the University of Mississippi. I teach the first-year writing sequence in FASTrack, a cohort-based program geared toward first generation and out-of-state students.


I’m a co-founder and faculty advisor for Missy: writing & art by UM’s LGBTQ+ students & allies. My current scholarship focuses on first-year writing, composition pedagogies, literate activities, and queer rhetorics.


I previously taught in the Writing & Rhetoric department at the University of Central Florida. I hold an MFA in Creative Writing and an MA in Journalism & Media Studies. My published writing can be found at TylerMTG.com


About this Site


This website serves as my professional portfolio for the Department of Writing & Rhetoric at the University of Mississippi.


The Service & Scholarship page describes my current projects, and the Teaching page contains my teaching philosophy, description of courses taught, memo documenting my teaching observations, and excerpts from student evaluations from the last calendar year. I’ve also included examples of student work, used with their permission.
